Horseriding club 'Sokol - Dupci'(KKSD) allows session bookings via a web application.

Private data required by the web application:

Your name, surname, username and password.

Your private data processing:

The application uses your private data to book a horse at a specific time.

The application uses your private data to disallow accidental double bookings.

Your requested booking has to be approved by the administrator.

Barring your account password, the Administrator is the only person with full access to your complete private data array.

Registered users are allowed access to the bookings timetable. The actual identifiers(name, surname) linking specific persons to their corresponding bookings are withheld from this view.

Expired bookings, be they approved or only requested are automatically deleted.

You may remove your requested bookings which have not yet been accepted.

Approved bookings may only be cancelled 24 hours in advance.

Private and public messages:

The administrator may send you private and/or public messages.

All private and public messages are automatically deleted after 7 days.

You may remove your own private messages.

Public messages cannot be removed by the end user.

Your profile:

You may delete your own profile.

Account deletion entails the automatic and immediate erasure of your private data array, barring any private data which may or may not be contained within the public messages.

In case of forgotten login data(username, password) you may petition the administrator for a new profile creation or deletion.

The administrator retains the privilege of removing your profile for any undisclosed reason.

Within the framework of your profile, you are allowed access to your data, including:

Name, surname, username, password, private and public messages, booking timetable data.

Horseriding club 'Sokol Dupci'(KKSD) is neither responsible, nor liable for any damages incurred by the potential private data loss.
